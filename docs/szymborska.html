Szymborska

This project is a tribute to Wisława Szymborska, the beloved Nobel Prize-winning Polish poet celebrated for her unique blend of humor and irony. The poster design draws inspiration from Szymborska's whimsical side and a place she affectionately referred to as 'Szymborska's drawer,' a nod to its cozy, small size. The typography featured in the poster is custom-designed, taking cues from the distinctive writing on the cover of her book 'Rymowanki.' This poster encapsulates the essence of Szymborska's literary charm, offering a visual homage to her distinctive voice and personality as a cherished literary figure.
