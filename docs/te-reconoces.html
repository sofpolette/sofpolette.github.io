¿Te reconoces?

This project focuses on an advertising campaign for the NOVA program by NGO Proyecto Hombre Canarias. The primary objective is to prevent addiction in minors and, crucially, to encourage them to seek help if they find themselves in challenging situations. The campaign features pixelated portraits of teenagers, symbolizing the distortion addiction can bring to one's life. The tagline, 'Do you recognize yourself?' prompts viewers to ponder the impact of addiction on their perception of reality and, more importantly, on their sense of self. This campaign aims to raise awareness and prompt essential conversations about addiction prevention among young audiences.
