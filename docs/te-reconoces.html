¿Te reconoces? (Do you recognize yourself?) is a campaign that aims to prevent drug addiction in minors.

Main goal:
Choose a specific NGO program and develop an intermedia advertising campaign to promote it.

Concept:
Through pixelating portraits of teenagers, I aimed to symbolize the distortion addiction can bring to one's life.

Tagline:
The tagline aids the image, prompting viewers to ponder the impact of addiction on their perception of reality and their sense of self.

After ideating the concept, it was adapted by me and my teammates to different formats and medias. The assets I designed are featured above, while the full campaign is below.
