Animal, plant, fungi is an illustration project that captures canarian nature in an unexpected way.

Main goal:
Create a triptic of scenes from using Otl Aicher's iconic illustrations, particularly for the city of Ulm.

Subject:
I chose the Canary Islands since they are known for their unique and diverse ecosystems, providing inspiration for a visually compelling journey. the farm.

Stylistic choices
The black and white color scheme as well as the geometric style capture the essence of the places they're inspired by while showing a different side of them.
