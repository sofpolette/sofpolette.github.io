Poster

Poster promoting EO, a film that explores the intricate relationship between animals and people through the eyes of a donkey in rural Poland.

Main goal:
Express the film's theme in a subtle, simple and clear manner.

How?
Through a euphemistic approach, I meant to show the contrast between pleasant and stark realities that the film portrays.

Stylistic choices:
The colorful but rough look of the poster is meant to mimic the visual style and feeling of the film. I used handmade lettering to aid the concept.
