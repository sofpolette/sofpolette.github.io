No Humanos is a crowdfunding initiative carried out in conjunction with the CREASD platform, aiming to raise funds for Finca Arkadia, a farm animal sanctuary located in Tenerife. Three designs inspired by the inhabitants of the farm were designed, manufactured and sold. The initial fundraising target set by the stakeholders was exceeded 2.3 times, demonstrating the overwhelming support and compassion of the community.
