Client
Branding

Gutiérrez Ledesma Abogados is a Law Firm based in Tejina, Tenerife.

Main goal:
Craft a visual identity that embodies the firm's values, experience and professionalism.

About the brand:
The main values of the brand include tradition, expertise and a commitment to justice.

Results:
The monogram merges 'G' and 'L' from the founder's surname, utilizing Greek equivalents to honor Greek symbolism. This also subtly resembles the letter 'm', representing the initial for the founder's name.
