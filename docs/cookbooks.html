Editorial

'Mom and Dad' is an exploration of my own heritage through family recipes and traditions.

Main goal:
Bridge the geographical and cultural gaps between both sides of my family through a compilation of recipes and traditions.

The recipes:
The handwritten recipes are the main focus of these two books, showcasing the different handwriting, textures and colors that different family members and friends used through the years.

Stylistic choices:
Even though both books share a similar visual language, they are distinguished through typography, color, and the composition of the images.
