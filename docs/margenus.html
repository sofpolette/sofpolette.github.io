Branding

Margenus is an emerging fashion brand on a mission to redefine luxury for professional women.

Main goal:
The project aims to brand an emerging fashion firm. I seeked to capture the essence of the designer's unique approach to luxury fashion and appeal to their target audience.

About the brand:
The brand's core values include elegance, boldness, and sustainability, with a distinctive emphasis on upcycling to create one-of-a-kind pieces that celebrate individuality, contrast and reinvention.

Results:
The naming (margin + Venus) is inspired by the target audience, women that follow their own path and stand out. The symbol shows the cycle of reinvention that both the clothes and the consumer go through throughout their life.
