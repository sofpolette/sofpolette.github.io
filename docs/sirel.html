Sirel is a beauty salon for pets and their human friends to enjoy, look and feel their best together.

Main goal:
Develop a brand identity for a niche business model.

Core values:
The brand values include natural, high quality treatments, personalized care, and heartfelt interactions.

Results:
Sirel stems from the word 'mermaid' in spanish (sirena), which serves as a symbol of beauty. The logotype is inspired by this same concept.
